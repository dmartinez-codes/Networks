A WAN connects multiple smaller networks like LANs across large areas such as cities, countries, or worldwide.

A PAN connects electronic devices within a person's immediate range, like phones, earbuds, and smartwatches.

A MAN connects multiple LANs across a city or large campus, larger than a LAN but smaller than a WAN.

A LAN connects devices in small areas like homes, offices, or school labs.
